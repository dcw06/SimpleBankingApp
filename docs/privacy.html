Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

FinWise ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates this application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information collection, use, and protection.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Facebook Account Information: When you sign in with Facebook, we receive your public profile (name and email address only). We do not request or store any other Facebook data.
  • Account Data: Account balances, transaction history, and account names you create
  • Bank Connection Data: When linking Bank of America via Plaid, we receive account information through secure Plaid integration (we never store your banking credentials)
  • Device Information: Basic device information for troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the FinWise service
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To process deposits and transfers
  • To improve and optimize the application
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data encryption for sensitive information
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all communications
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Local data storage with encryption

5. Third-Party Integrations

Google Authentication: We use Google OAuth for secure sign-in. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Facebook Authentication: We use Facebook Login for secure sign-in. We only request your public profile (name and email). See Facebook's Data Policy: https://www.facebook.com/policy.php

Plaid Integration: We use Plaid to securely link your bank account. Plaid never shares your bank credentials with us. See Plaid's privacy policy: https://plaid.com/legal

6. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your computer in an encrypted JSON file at:

~/.FinWise/bank_data.json

We do not maintain servers or upload your data to external services (except through Plaid for bank connections).

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can access all your data stored in the app
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export: You can export your data in standard formats

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of major changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through GitHub:

GitHub: https://github.com/dcw06/SimpleBankingApp/issues

10. Compliance

FinWise complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for EU users
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) for California users
  • Standard data protection practices for all users
